

# Huwag KuKURAP: Probing Public Records with Data Science
### **UP Data Science Society | ACLE 2026 Workshop**

---

## **About the Workshop**
This notebook is the official teaching material for the **UP Data Science Society's 2026 ACLE Workshop**. Designed as a crash course in investigative data analytics, "Huwag KuKURAP" teaches participants how to bypass political rhetoric and go straight to the data. 

Through this material, you will learn to extract and analyze public records, uncover patterns of possible government corruption, and use data literacy as a weapon for a more transparent future.
